About Us

Welcome to Carreker Cosmetics, where beauty meets passion! We are dedicated to providing you with high-quality cosmetics that enhance your natural beauty and make you feel confident in your own skin.

Founder and CEO: Jamair Carreker

Carreker Cosmetics was founded by Jamair Carreker, a visionary entrepreneur with a passion for beauty and empowerment. Jamair, currently 33 years old, is not only a skilled business owner but also an experienced MS math teacher. With a strong background in education and a deep understanding of numbers, Jamair brings a unique perspective to the world of cosmetics.

Our Mission: We Care For You

At Carreker Cosmetics, our mission is simple: to care for you. We believe that beauty is more than just a superficial concept; it's about embracing your individuality and feeling confident in your own skin. That's why we carefully curate each product to ensure it meets our high standards of quality, safety, and effectiveness.

Based in Brooklyn, New York

Although our roots lie in New Jersey, Carreker Cosmetics proudly calls Brooklyn, New York, home. Inspired by the vibrant energy and diverse culture of this bustling city, we strive to create products that cater to individuals of all backgrounds and skin types.
